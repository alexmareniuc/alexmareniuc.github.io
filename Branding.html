Rebranding Mifne România

The Mifne Romania project brings the Mifne method to Romania, setting up the first early diagnosis and intensive early treatment centre for babies identified at risk of developing autism and their families. The project is run through the Turning Point in Early Autism Association, a non-profit organization established in early 2021, in the city of Piatra Neamț, with the mission to improve the quality of life of families with children with autism.
