About me

Hey there! I'm Alex, and I'm all about creating cool stuff with pixels and a camera. I studied Journalism at Bucharest University, and my bachelor's project was a visual makeover for a beer brand called Kimpolung – it was quite a hit!

Right now, I'm tackling a master's degree at Faculty of Political Science, Administration, and Communication at Babeș Bolyai University.

My journey started at dploy agency, where I worked as a copywriter. But guess what? Graphic design stole my heart, and now I'm waving my creative wand as a graphic designer and photographer at dploy, always up for new collaborations.

For almost four years, I've been crafting pixels and capturing moments, specializing in making brands look awesome. Lately, I've been adding some motion magic and a bit of UI/UX finesse.
